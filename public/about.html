About Word Giggles

What is Word Giggles?

Word Giggles is a free, playful word-learning site. Type any word (or say it out loud using the microphone button), and Word Giggles instantly generates a clear definition, a similar word, a silly joke, a fun example sentence, and a matching animated GIF to bring the word to life. It's designed to make building vocabulary fun for kids, students, and word-lovers of any age.

How to play

  1. Type a word into the input box, or tap the microphone icon and say a word out loud.
  2. Press the Make button.
  3. Word Giggles will show you the word's meaning, a similar word, a joke, and an example sentence, along with a fun GIF.
  4. Tap any of the speaker icons to have that section read aloud to you.

Why we built it

We wanted a quick, lighthearted way to explore new words without feeling like a chore. By pairing definitions with jokes, example sentences, and GIFs, Word Giggles turns vocabulary practice into something you actually want to click "Make" on again.
